Pick Up in Store

There’s a new service in town: select your convenient pickup store, most in-stock items are available for pickup in just 2 business hours!
Just follow these simple steps:

  • A
  • B
  • C

STEP 1

Select your favourite frame online and select ‘Pickup in store’ in the cart, it’s free.

STEP 2

Select your convenient pickup store and check opening hours, most in-stock items are available for pickup in 2h!*

STEP 3

Proceed to checkout, we’ll let you know as soon as your pair is available for collection. No payment request in store.

*While stocks last. In-stock items available for same day pickup are marked as ‘COLLECT TODAY’: they will be ready for you in just 2 business hours, including weekends. Orders will be kept in the store for up to 1 week for pick-up. Items not in stock are still eligible for free pickup in store, you can select your convenient location and we will ship a fresh pair in express shipping.
